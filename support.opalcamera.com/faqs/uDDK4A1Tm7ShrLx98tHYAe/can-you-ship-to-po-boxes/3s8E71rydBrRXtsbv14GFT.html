Can You Ship to PO Boxes?

At Opal, we strive to provide the best shipping experience for our customers. However, there are certain limitations to our shipping capabilities. Here's what you need to know about shipping to PO Boxes

📦 Shipping to PO Boxes:

Unfortunately, for C1 orders we do not ship to PO Boxes. We ask to provide a physical address for shipping to ensure that your order reaches you without any issues.

 

However, for Tadpole orders, we are able to ship to any PO box address!

In Conclusion:

We understand the convenience of PO Boxes for many customers, but due to our shipping logistics, we're unable to deliver to them. If you have any questions or need assistance with your shipping address, our support team is always here to help. Reach out to us at support@opalcamera.com.
