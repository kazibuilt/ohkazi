Sound.

Get ready for your
audio close-up.

The Opal C1 comes with
MicMesh™,
an array of beamforming microphones that find and focus on the sound of your voice. And with intelligent noise cancellation, every ding, drop and dog bark is filtered out to get an audio experience that clearly speaks for itself.

  • A revolution in resonance.

    Using inputs from a disparate mesh of microphones the Opal C1 is able to capture a depth of frequency specifically tuned to your voice. For a sound that sounds more like you.

  • Noise awareness.

    With MicMesh, the core microphones hone in on the speaker’s voice while the backward-facing, ambient microphones capture and filter out the surrounding environment.
